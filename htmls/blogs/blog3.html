Where to begin

At RP, our main aim is to celebrate and connect with women all over the world through our love of fitness and living healthy lifestyles. Some of you have been a part of our sisterhood since we first began and others could still be deciding to join. Fitness can be overwhelming, as there are many misconceptions around the industry, as well as many other sources of information that contradict each other.

You want to start, you just don’t know how or where to begin...

Sis, you’re not alone. We’re a family at RP- so we are here to guide you in your journey, whether you’re a beginner or have been with us before, to help you achieve your goals!

As sisters we have always motivated each other over time, now we want to share our sisterhood with you! There is never enough room to support and love your sisters and cheer them on. We are just average girls seeking the best versions of ourselves and we hope to share all our time and knowledge with you too!

Start Slow

Starting is always the hardest part, so start by doing the one thing that will make you feel good every single day. This could be simply going for a walk, reaching your water targets every day, speaking kindly to yourself, putting on gym clothes and doing a home workout or actually going to the gym, or even as simple as cleaning up your diet. You don’t have to do EVERYTHING all at once. Start by creating at least one healthy habit and stick to it and build up from there. Mindset will play a huge role in sticking to this habit, so attach it to something deeply-routed within you to start, try and continue.

Define Your Goal

Establish WHY you want to get fit and live a healthy lifestyle. We do not believe in quick fixes or short term pleasures, we are here for the long run, and you should be as well! Your goal should be attached to something that YOU want to achieve and not a societal goal, otherwise you will struggle to reach it and most often, won’t reap the benefits or results that you could have if you truly kept your WHY to something attached to you.

Create a routine and follow a plan

This is so important, yet gets missed by so many people. It can be so overwhelming and demotivating when you want to reach a goal and you have no idea how to get there or you’re trying so many different things and they’re just not working out for you. So many of us hinder our own results by not following any structure- so do not fall into this trap! Your body needs consistency. Sit down and allocate time to workout, prep your meals and get in adequate amount of hours of sleep in order to perform your best. We all live busy lives but don’t let that keep you from creating a healthy routine, because it can be done. Commit to yourself and your goals and let the magic happen. The best thing about this, is all you need to do is show up for yourself!

Get creative

Naturally, we need a variety of things to stay motivated. Why not try having fun while you’re working hard? Try out different workouts until you pick something you truly enjoy. Grab a friend to workout with or challenge your family to do things with you! Working out does not need to be this serious routine element in our lives. It’s what us as sisters used to do to spend time together, at the same time working hard and reaching our own individual goals.

Reach out to others for support

It can be difficult if you’re on this journey alone- but like we said at the beginning, you are never alone! We are all on the same journey as you, and knowing that there is a whole community of women who are as well, is super-helpful in staying motivated and committed to your goals. Sharing your positive and realistic experience can help others who were in the same position as you were, to continue working hard and to never give up on their goals. So share your story with us, as we embark in sharing ours with you.

That’s why we are here.

Welcome to our Sisterhood.
