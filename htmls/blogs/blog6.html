TIME TO DETOX

We have all seen the hypes of detox skinny teas, juice cleanses or smoothie diets out in the market, but how much of all of this is effective?

At RP we believe that we have all the tools we need to detox our bodies, without going to any outrageous extremes!

HOW DOES THE BODY DETOX?

When we detox our bodies remove these impurities and toxins from our blood in most of our organs such as our liver, kidneys, intestines, lungs and skin. These systems however can become blocked, which means the toxins aren’t removed from our bodies as they should be, which will have a negative effect on how our bodies function.

There are a few ways we can detox to remove waste:

  • 1. Resetting the organs and revitalizing cells through diet
  • 2. Stimulating the liver to excrete toxins
  • 3. Stimulating detoxification through the intestines, kidneys, and skin
  • 4. Improving circulation of the blood and lymphatic systems
  • 5. Nourishing the body with the correct nutrients
  • 6. Removing inflammation triggers from food

SIGNS INDICATING DETOXIFICATION IS NEEDED

To put it in simple terms, we all need to detox. We are all exposed to toxins, and processed foods and alcohol enter into our bodies from time to time and it’s important to get rid of those unnecessary wastes.

Below are some common signals that you may be due for a detox.

  • Repeated fatigue
  • Slow digestion
  • Skin flare ups or irritations
  • Frequent allergies or congestions
  • Dark circles under eyes
  • Head fog or difficulty in keeping concentration
  • Constant muscle / joint soreness
  • Pain in lower and Upper legs
  • Recurring headaches
  • Sleep deprived
  • Sugar cravings
  • Water retention
  • Inability to lose fat effectively

METHODS TO DETOXING NATURALLY

ITS TIME TO HYDRATE PROPERLY

To help your body flush out the build-up of toxins, it is vital that you supply your body with sufficient hydration; at least 2-3L per day. To further enhance this, add lemon and apple cider throughout the day to boost the alkaline of the water. Both lemon and apple cider have detoxing and cleansing properties. Staying hydrated will not only cleanses the body of toxins but it will aid boosting metabolism, clear up skin, and assist in organ functionality.

CLEANSE

The best way to move the toxins that have been built up in our bodies is to keep our bowels moving. We should be going to the bathroom at least once or twice each day. If you aren’t going to the bathroom frequently, try adding some natural enhancements such as gingers, berries, and chai or flax seeds. The lemon and apple cider mentioned above will also help with this.

FOOD

The foundation of your diet when detoxing should be high-quality (grass-fed or organic) protein, fats and non-starchy vegetables. You want to be careful that you aren’t spiking your blood sugar or insulin too often whilst detoxing.

Make a conscious effort, to focus on consuming organic produce or fresh products. This will help to eliminate a lot of the toxins, hormones, and antibiotics that we consume via food.
